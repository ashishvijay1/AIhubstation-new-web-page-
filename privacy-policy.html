Introduction

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide you with the best experience while using our services. Specifically, we may use your information to:

How We Protect Your Information

We prioritize the security of your personal information. AI HUB Station uses a variety of technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. This includes:

  • Encrypting payment information using secure socket layer (SSL) technology.
  • Storing your data on secure servers with restricted access.
  • Implementing two-factor authentication (2FA) for user accounts, where applicable.

However, no data transmission over the internet is guaranteed to be 100% secure, and we cannot ensure the complete safety of your information. You are encouraged to use strong, unique passwords and safeguard your account credentials.
